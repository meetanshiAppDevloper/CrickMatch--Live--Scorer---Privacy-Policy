Privacy Policy for CrickMatch Live Scorer

Last Updated: November 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CrickMatch Live Scorer, developed by MEETANSHI TECHNOLOGIES LLP. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles user information and ensures a secure experience for all users.

2. Information Collection and Use

We respect your privacy and minimize data handling to only what is essential for app functionality.

  • The app provides Google Sign-In and Guest Login options. We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data beyond the authentication process managed securely by Google Firebase.
  • We do not collect or store sensitive information such as name, email, phone number, contacts, or location.
  • All match data (teams, scores, and game history) is stored securely in your Firebase account, under your full control.

3. Permissions

The app may request minimal permissions for its functionality:

  • Internet Access: Required for Google Sign-In, Firebase sync, and live score sharing via Dynamic Links.
  • Storage Access (optional): Only used for exporting and sharing match PDFs that you create locally. Files are not uploaded to our servers.

4. Live Score Sharing

CrickMatch Live Scorer allows users to share live match scores via Firebase Dynamic Links. These links display live score data only. No personal information is included or shared through these links. Recipients can view the live scoreboard but cannot access or modify other user data.

5. Data Security

All data used by the app is handled via Google Firebase with industry-standard security measures. We do not sell, share, or transmit personal data to third-party servers. Users retain full control of their data and can delete match data from their account at any time.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect personal data, CrickMatch Live Scorer is compliant with applicable child protection regulations, including COPPA and GDPR.

7. Advertising and Analytics

Currently, the app does not include advertisements or third-party analytics SDKs. If we introduce such services in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before implementation and users will be notified.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer user data to third parties. All data processing is done locally on the user’s device or via Google’s secure Firebase infrastructure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or changes to the app. The updated version will always be available here with a new “Last Updated” date.
